Dhananjay

Functional Materials Laboratory, Central University of Kerala

I am a PhD scholar at Central University of Kerala, working on the synthesis, characterization, and applications of fluorescent materials. My research focuses on understanding fluorescence mechanisms via computational tools and developing new materials for sensing applications.
